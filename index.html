CrystalsCollector

- You will be shown a random number in the white box. The object of the game is to match this "Desired Score".

- Clicking on any of the crystal images will add a certain amount of points to your current score, shown in the teal box. You will not be shown the value of the crystals. You will have to rely on memorization from clicking each crystal.

- You will win the game, only if your current score happens to match the number displayed in the white box.

- You will lose this game if your number soars above the desired score.

- The game will reset if you either win, or lose.

- You will see a new number shown in the white box, after your won or loss. Keep in mind as well, each of the crystal images will have four new values (these will not be shown as well).
