CrystalsCollector

- You will be shown a random number in the white box. The object of the game is to match thise number.

- Clicking on any of the crystal images will add a certain amount of points to your total score, shown in the teal box. You will not be shown the value of the crystals. You will have to rely on memorization from clicking each crystal.

- You will win the game, only if your total score happens to match the number displayed in the white box.

- You will lose this game if you number soars above the number in the white box.

- The game will reset if you either win, or lose.

- You will see a new number shown in the white box, after your won or loss. Keep in mind as well, each of the crystal images will have four new values (these will not be shown as well).
